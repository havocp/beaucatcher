CasbahBackend

class CasbahBackend extends MongoBackend

CasbahBackend is final with a private constructor - there's no way to create one directly. However, a CasbahBackendProvider exposes a CasbahBackend and you may want to use provider.backend.underlyingConnection, underlyingDB, or underlyingCollection to get at Casbah methods directly.
